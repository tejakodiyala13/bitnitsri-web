About Us

The BIS Club at NIT Srinagar is dedicated to spreading awareness about standards and quality. Inspired by the Bureau of Indian Standards, the club organizes events, workshops, and activities to promote standardization and innovation among students. The club is supported by NIT Srinagar and works in collaboration with BIS India.
