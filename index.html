About Us

The BIS Club at NIT Srinagar works to spread awareness about quality, standardization, and innovation through events, seminars, and workshops, nurturing future leaders of quality-driven industries.

Mission

Our mission is to create a knowledgeable community that understands the importance of standardization. We strive to integrate quality consciousness among students, preparing them to contribute meaningfully to various sectors nationally and globally.

Vision

We envision fostering a generation of students who actively apply quality and standardization principles to real-world problems, innovating responsibly and shaping the future of industries through excellence and sustainable practices.
